NOORA GUO

Hello~! I am a game developer student and a hobbyist illustrator from Espoo, Finland. I'm currently doing my final year in Metropolia University of Applied Sciences, studying ICT and majoring in game development. I specialize in art and storytelling, but I also enjoy coding and problem-solving. Before studying in Metropolia, I studied graphic design and illustration for a year in Lahti Academy of Liberal and Fine Arts. Here in my portfolio, I've collected some of my favorites from the projects I've worked on.

Feel free to contact me using the methods at the bottom of this page!
